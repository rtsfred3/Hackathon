Welcome to myExposure!

COVID-19 has changed the way many of us live our lives. There is
risk in anything we do and it's important to observe the things we do daily.
myExposure will allow you to do just that. Complete the questionnaire and find out
what your exposure risk is. Relax, use this information to make smarter decisions
and compare your score with your social circle.
